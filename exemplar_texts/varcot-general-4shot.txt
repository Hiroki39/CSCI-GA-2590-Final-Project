Q: There are c0 trees in the grove. Grove workers will plant trees in the grove today. After they are done, there will be c1 trees. How many trees did the grove workers plant today?
A: There are c0 trees originally. Then there were c1 trees after some more were planted. So there must have been c1 - c0 new trees. 
new_trees = c1 - c0
The answer is new_trees.

Q: If there are c0 cars in the parking lot and c1 more cars arrive, how many cars are in the parking lot?
A: There are originally c0 cars. c1 more cars arrive. The total is c0 + c1. 
total_car = c0 + c1
The answer is total_car.

Q: Leah had c0 chocolates and her sister had c1. If they ate c2, how many pieces do they have left in total?
A: Originally, Leah had c0 chocolates. Her sister had c1.  So in total they had c0 + c1 chocolates. 
total_chocolates = c0 + c1
After eating c2, they had total_chocolates - c2 chocolates remaining. 
remaining_chocolates = total_chocolates - c2
The answer is remaining_chocolates.

Q: Jason had c0 lollipops. He gave Denny some lollipops. Now Jason has c1 lollipops. How many lollipops did Jason give to Denny?
A: Jason started with c0 lollipops. Then he had c1 after giving some to Denny. So he gave Denny c1 - c0 lollipops.
lollipops_given = c1 - c0
The answer is lollipops_given.
